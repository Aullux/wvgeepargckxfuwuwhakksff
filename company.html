THE UNIVERSE THAT EVERYONE DREAMS OF

Your world hangs out here. Eighred is a universe that more than a hundred million people use to spend time with their friends and communities.

OUR STORY

The point of Eighred is to give people the opportunity to create environments in their own lives where they feel that they belong. We want you to be able to establish sincere relationships with your friends and community near you or on the other side of the world. An environment where you can feel original, reliable, fun and belonging. These are exactly the values that unite our users and employees at Eighred.
Prologue
Eighred challenged to solve a big problem: how to be free with friends around the world while playing online games? Our founder Akif Grape's love for computer games and the value he attaches to the friendships formed while playing games. At that time, the entire world developed for this work was limited, unreliable and complex. Akif Grape knew that he could develop a better service that serves to create shared memories and provides a sense of being together through playing games.
Chapter One
So, Akif Grape began to implement Eighred in 2021. Eighred has won the love of many people from all over the world. Thanks to Eighred, there had been a universe beyond the game with friends. Friends did not break away from each other thanks to various communities. The technology was complex, but the goal was simple: to make Eighred a home that was used to meet communities and friends.
How are we doing now?
With up to 4 hours of working time on the Eighred platform every day, millions of people are creating environments for their friends and communities. Eighred is now a place where the whole world talks, hangs out and develops their dreams. Eighred allows everyone to create an environment where they will feel like they belong; just like for Akif Grape.
